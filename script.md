# Script


  - This is odyssiphus. Its a roguelite dungeon crawler based on the Odyssey. You Play as odysseus and have to fight your way through a dungeon to return home to your kingdom of ithaca.

  - The home screen contains a list of saved games, a button to start a new game, a settings page, and a quit button. The settings page contains options for audio.

  - The game starts with a cutscene of odysseus on his ship, and as you leave the ship you are put into the dungeon. The dungeon is procedurally generated using a random walk algorithm. The dungeon contains 5 types of rooms. The starting room, enemy rooms, treasure rooms, mini boss rooms, and the final boss room. 

  - If the player dies (demonstrate dying) they are put back into the ship and all the rooms and enemies are regenerated.

  - The enemy rooms spawn different types of enemies that the player has to fight. It uses a state machine to control all entities in the game.
     - Ryan will talk more about this

  - The mini boss rooms spawn a mini boss which is a suped up version of a normal enemy. 

  - the treasure room contain a trinket that you can hold in your inventory. Trinkets give you a passive bonus that can help you on your run.
     - Grayson will talk more about this
  
  - After beating the final boss, you are put into a cutscene of odysseus returning home to ithaca. and the game ends
